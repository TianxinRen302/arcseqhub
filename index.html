Arcseqhub - ancestral introgression analysis

This project is to analyzing the DNA introgression of the archaic humans and modern human to reveal the status of archaic human in the evolution of modern human.

In this project, we used complete genome of Neanderthal and Denisovan from previous study as the genetics source of archaic human[1, 2]; besides, we used different reference genome to analyze introgression to reveal the influence of reference genome.

We use a novel probabilistic method called IBDmix to identify introgressed hominin sequences in this project[3] , is a way doesn't need a modern reference genome to analyze archaic introgression.

We hope to find interesting introgression events in different scales between archaic human and modern human, to get insights about human evolution, and promote related studies.

Reference:

  • [1]: Prüfer K, Racimo F, Patterson N, et al. The complete genome sequence of a Neanderthal from the Altai Mountains.
  • [2]: Reich D, Green RE, Kircher M, et al. Genetic history of an archaic hominin group from Denisova Cave in Siberia.
  • [3]: Chen L, Wolf AB, Fu W, Li L, Akey JM. Identifying and Interpreting Apparent Neanderthal Ancestry in African Individuals.

Archaic introgression is the key to reveal human evolution

Previous studies have revealed that admixture of ancient DNA shaped patterns of human genomic variation; especially we discovered multiple admixtures events with archaic hominins throughout human history; thus, this project is to reveal archaic introgression further, and provides materials for downstream study and also possible insights on human evolution.

What we are doing

Here, we applied a novel method-IBDmix to identify archaic introgression without modern reference genome, with the complete genome from Denisovan and Altai Neanderthal.

We also build this website-arcseqhub, with locally developed tool, to display archaic introgression events from different scale.

Also, data is freely to download for downstream analysis.
